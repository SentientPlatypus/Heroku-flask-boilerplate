Welcome to the Scroll Nudge Experiment

In this experiment, you will be asked to find buyer requests with a value greater than a certain threshold.

You will play 20 rounds (+5 practice rounds). Each round consists of 100 requests.

If you find a request with a value greater than the threshold, click the "Select" button.

If you make a mistake, you will be penalized with a 1-minute screen freeze.

Click "Next" to begin the practice rounds.
